About Us

Teqpadi is an innovative Nigerian tech startup based in Lagos, committed to transforming the way consumers repair, buy, and swap their tech devices. Our platform combines a reliable verification system, geolocation features, and a transparent rating system to create a trustworthy and efficient marketplace for all your tech needs. At Teqpadi, we focus on simplifying gadget repairs in Nigeria. We offer expert, hassle-free repair services and device swaps directly to your doorstep, saving you time and effort. Whether you're looking to repair your device, swap it for a new one, or purchase a gadget, Teqpadi is your trusted partner for all things tech. Motto: Repair, Swap, Buy – Teqpadi, your Guy.

Why Choose Us?

We believe in making technology easy to use and maintain. Whether it's fixing a broken screen, swapping your phone, or providing expert advice on your gadgets, we have you covered.
